Overview

The Waffle House Index is an informal metric used by the Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) to assess the severity of a disaster based on the status of Waffle House restaurants in the affected area.

Categories

  • Green: Full menu available, restaurant has power and supplies.
  • Yellow: Limited menu available, indicating power outages or supply shortages.
  • Red: Restaurant is closed, indicating severe damage or unsafe conditions.
