Waffle House Index

The Waffle House Index is an informal metric used to measure the severity of disasters, particularly hurricanes, in the United States. It is based on the operational status of Waffle House restaurants following a disaster.

Index Levels

  • Green: Full menu available, minimal impact.
  • Yellow: Limited menu, power outages or supply issues.
  • Red: Waffle House is closed, indicating severe disaster impact.

Significance

FEMA and emergency responders use the Waffle House Index as an indicator of disaster recovery. Since Waffle House has a strong disaster preparedness plan, closures signal extreme conditions.
